# **App Name**: NairaMemeTrader

## Core Features:

- Meme Ticker Creation: Allow users to create meme tickers with a unique name, description, and initial details.
- Ticker Listing and Search: Display a list of available meme tickers, sortable by popularity (market cap), and searchable by name or keywords.
- Naira-Denominated Trading: Enable users to buy and sell meme tickers using Naira (NGN).
- Real-time Price Chart: Provide real-time price charts for each meme ticker, visualizing price movements and trading volume.
- Market Cap Calculation: Calculate and display the market capitalization for each meme ticker based on its current price and outstanding supply.
- Profit/Loss Tracking: Track user's profit or loss on each meme ticker they hold.
- Trending Memes: Identify and showcase the most popular meme tickers based on recent trading activity and price appreciation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant Purple (#9400D3) for excitement and uniqueness.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0), providing a clean, modern look.
- Accent color: Electric Green (#7CFC00) for highlighting positive changes and call-to-actions, signaling growth and gains.
- Body and headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a sans-serif for a techy feel.
- Use relevant and engaging icons for meme tickers and platform features, with a touch of humor.
- A clean and intuitive layout, emphasizing real-time price charts and trading functionality.
- Subtle animations to enhance the user experience, such as price changes, market trends and when creating meme tickers
